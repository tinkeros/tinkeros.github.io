Baremetal Installs


Machines marked with a * are also potentially good for TempleOS installs

It is recommended to pick only machines with real PS/2 ports. Working PS/2 
emulation from USB can depend on particular BIOS versions and is never 
guarenteed to work.

  Dell OptiPlex 7050 MFF

  Dell OptiPlex 9010 SFF

  Lenovo ThinkCentre M79 * w/PS/2
