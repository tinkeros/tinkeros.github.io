h/F1        - help
q/ESC       - quit
s/SHIFT-ESC - smash cube and quit

Arrow keys  - Rotate entire cube 

Rotate face front left / back right:
f - Rotate front left clockwise
F - Rotate front left counter-clockwise
b - Rotate back right clockwise
B - Rotate back right counter-clockwise

Rotate face front right / back left:
r - Rotate front right clockwise
R - Rotate front right counter-clockwise
l - Rotate back left clockwise
L - Rotate back left counter-clockwise

Rotate face top (upper) / bottom (down):
u - Rotate the upper face clockwise
U - Rotate the upper face counter-clockwise
d - Rotate the downward face clockwise
D - Rotate the downward face counter-clockwise

Cheater multi-step keys:
o - Orient the top cube closet to you. (RDrd)x2
c - Permute top to make cross. (assumes bottom 2 layers done) (fruRUF)
p - Permute the top 3 corners farthest from you. (urULuRUl)
e - Exchange top left front middle with the top left back middle. (ruRuruuRu)
m - Move middle top left cube to left middle layer on the right. (urURUFuf)
M - Move middle top left cube to left middle layer on the left. (ULulufUF)

Beginners cube solve guide with help from cheat key algorithms:

1) Complete any outter face layer. (Hint do top, then e/p/u/U maybe useful!)
2) Move complete layer to bottom arrows and align so middle is correct. d/D
3) Use u/U and m/M to complete middle layer.
4) Use u/U and c to make cross on top layer.
5) Use u/U and e then u/U and p to place top cubes in correct locations.
6) Use u/U and o to re-orient the top layer corners to complete.
